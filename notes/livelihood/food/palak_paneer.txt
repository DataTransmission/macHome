
Palak Paneer (http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/aarti-sequeira/saag-paneer-spinach-with-indian-cheese-recipe.html)
------------
I. turmeric*1ts, cayenne_pepper*1/2ts, salt*3ts, veg_oil*1/2ts, paneer*12oz


II. onion*1(finely_chopped), ginger*1inch(minced), garlic*4(minced),
    green_serrano_chile*1(finely_chopped), 


III. garam_masala*1/2ts, coriander*2ts(ground), cumin*1ts(ground), salt*1ts 

IV. spinach*16oz(frozen_chopped)

V. yogurt*1/2cup

1. whisk I. with paneer tossed -> stir fry until paneer brown -> plate
2. stir fry II. -> add III. and stir (5 min)
3. blend IV. and add to 2. (5min) -> turn off heat
4. add V. to 3. mix well -> add 1.



Paneer
------
Cheesecloth, whole_milk*8cup, lemon_juice*1/4cup(fresh_squeezed)

1. line a large colander with 2 layers of cheesecloth 
2. heat the milk until gently boiled
3. add the lemon juice and turn off heat, stir until curd.
4. pour on cheesecloth and wait for drainage -> put a weight on top to further drain -> put in
   fridge (20 min)
